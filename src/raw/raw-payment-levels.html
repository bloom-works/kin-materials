Find out how much money you'll receive to support the child in your care. {% Callout "note", "undefined" %}In January 2024, DCYF updated its caregiver payment levels. [Learn more about why we made these changes](https://www.dcyf.wa.gov/services/foster-parenting/caregiver-supports-project/phase-one). {% endCallout %} ### Levels of caregiver supports Your monthly payment amount depends on the level or type of caregiver supports the child needs. Here are the different **levels** of caregiver supports: * **Level 1 (Basic care)**: The child does not have any identified health or behavioral needs. * **Level 2**: The child or youth is age 12 or older with no identified health or behavioral needs. * **Level 3**: The child or youth has a chronic health condition that may last at least a year. * **Level 4**: The child or youth has a developmental disability. * **Level 5**: The child or youth has both a chronic health condition and a developmental disability. * **Level 6**: The child or youth has a mental health diagnosis or a prescription for a mental health condition within the past 15 months. * **Level 7**: The child or youth has attempted suicide or self-harm, requiring medical care in the past 15 months, or has received crisis mental health services during that time. Learn more about [how we assign levels of caregiver supports (PDF)](https://www.dcyf.wa.gov/sites/default/files/pubs/CWP_0038.pdf). ### Monthly caregiver payment amount You can get basic foster care payments (**Level 1**) to reimburse you for food, clothing, shelter, and other caregiving costs. The payments are given monthly at a flat rate. They’re not an itemized reimbursement for individual caregiving costs. **Levels 2 to 7** are supplemental or extra payments you can get if you care for a child who has physical, developmental, behavioral, or mental health needs. Depending on the child’s age and level of caregiver supports, here’s your estimated monthly payment amount. #### Age 0 to 5 * **Level 1:** $722 * **Level 2:** N/A * **Level 3:** $1,407 * **Level 4:** $1,759.50 * **Level 5:** $2,092 * **Level 6:** $2,434.50 * **Level 7:** $2,777 #### Age 5 to 11 * **Level 1:** $846 * **Level 2:** N/A * **Level 3:** $1,531 * **Level 4:** $1,873.50 * **Level 5:** $2,216 * **Level 6:** $2,558.50 * **Level 7:** $2,901 #### Age 12 or older * **Level 1:** $860 * **Level 2:** $1,202.50 * **Level 3:** $1,545 * **Level 4:** $1,887.50 * **Level 5:** $2,230 * **Level 6:** $2,572.50 * **Level 7:** $2,951 #### Get help If you have questions about caregiver supports, email us at: [dcyf.caregiversupports@dcyf.wa.gov](mailto:dcyf.caregiversupports@dcyf.wa.gov). {% Callout "related" %} * [Payments and financial support](./payments.html) * [Get foster care payments](./payments-foster-care.html) {% endCallout %}
