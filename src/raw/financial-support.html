--- layout: layouts/rawAllCaregivers.njk title: Payments and financial support templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- We understand that the costs of caring for a child or youth can be overwhelming. There are several options for financial support that may help you. ## For licensed foster parents and licensed kinship caregivers only  The main source of financial support for caregivers is [monthly foster care payments](https://dcyf.wa.gov/services/foster-parenting/kinship-caregivers/roles-responsibilities/payments/foster-care-payments). These payments may cover food, clothing, shelter, and other caregiving expenses.  These payments are available to licensed foster parents and licensed kinship caregivers only.  [Learn more about monthly foster care payments](https://dcyf.wa.gov/services/foster-parenting/kinship-caregivers/roles-responsibilities/payments/foster-care-payments).  {% Callout "note", "Note" %} If you’re an unlicensed kinship caregiver with DCYF, or want to become one, we recommend becoming licensed so you can get monthly foster care payments. You can get a license after you complete background checks and the home study process. {% endCallout %} ## For licensed foster parents and all kinship caregivers There are some options for financial support for caregivers, regardless of whether you’re a licensed or unlicensed caregiver. These include transportation reimbursement from DCYF, and other help from community organizations and Washington state.  Learn more about other types of financial support [**add link**]. {% Callout "related", "Related content" %} * Get a license to become a foster parent [**add link**] * Requirements to become a kinship caregiver [**add link**] * Monthly payments for licensed caregivers [**add link**] {% endCallout %} {% Callout "note", "Read the regulations" %} For more information read the Washington Administrative Codes (WACs) related to these topics: For foster parents * WAC 110-148-1320: Foster family home licensing requirements For kinship caregivers * [WAC 110-149-0060](https://app.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=110-149-0060): Foster care maintenance payments {% endCallout %}
