--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: Drugs and alcohol templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- You must keep your child safe from drugs and alcohol. ### Access to drugs and alcohol Keep these substances stored away where your child cannot access them: * Alcohol * Marijuana * Nicotine products, including cigarettes, cigars, vapes, nicotine pouches, and chewing tobacco * Other drugs and paraphernalia DCYF can give you a lock if you need one. ### Personal use of drugs and alcohol Do not use drugs or alcohol in a way that affects your ability to care for your child.
