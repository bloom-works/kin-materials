--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: Drugs and alcohol templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- You must keep the child or youth in your care away from drugs and alcohol in your home. ### Access to drugs and alcohol Keep these substances stored away where the child or youth cannot access them: * Alcohol * Marijuana * Nicotine products, including cigarettes, cigars, vapes, nicotine pouches, and chewing tobacco * Other drugs and paraphernalia If you have any safety concerns about how to keep drugs or alcohol stored away from the child or youth, talk to your licensor. We can support you and can give you a lock if you need one. ### Personal use of drugs and alcohol Do not use drugs or alcohol in a way that affects your ability to safely care for the child or youth.
