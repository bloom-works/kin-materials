--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: Hearings and appeals templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- If we [deny, suspend, or revoke your kinship caregiver license](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/license-denials-suspensions-revocations), and you disagree with our decision, you have the right to an administrative hearing. You must send a written request for a hearing to the office of administrative hearings. Send your written request within 28 days of receiving a certified letter with our decision about your license. {% Callout "note", "Read the regulations" %} For more information read the Washington Administrative Codes (WACs) related to these topics: * WAC 110-149-0110: Applicants or licensed kinship caregivers' right to an administrative hearing {% endCallout %}
