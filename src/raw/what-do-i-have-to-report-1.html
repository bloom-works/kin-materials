Notify all of these **immediately and within 24 hours**: * Law enforcement or tribal law enforcement, and * National Center for Missing and Exploited Children at 1-800-843-5678, and * Your child's caseworker (or department intake, if you cannot contact your child’s caseworker directly, or it’s after normal business hours 2. Provide law enforcement and the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children with this information: * A recent photo of your child * A description of your child's physical features, including: * Height * Weight * Gender * Ethnicity * Race * Hair color * Eye color * Any other health or risk factors, including: * If your child is pregnant * Their prescription medications * If your child has suicidal tendencies * If your child is vulnerable to being sex trafficked 3. If at any time, you find out where your child is, or they return home, report this to the caseworker or department intake.
