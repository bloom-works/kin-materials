--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: What do I need permission for? templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- You need to get permission for certain aspects of the child or youth’s care. Follow the instructions on this page. ### The child or youth’s appearance Get permission from the child or youth's caseworker before you make major changes to the child’s appearance including, but not limited to: * Haircuts  * Changes to the color of their hair  * Piercings * Tattoos ### Indian children  If the child or youth is in the custody of a tribal court, get permission from the tribe to consent for their medical care. Consult with the tribe before the child or youth travels out of the state or country. {% Callout "related", "Related content" %} * Health and medicine * Indian children {% endCallout %} {% Callout "note", "Read the regulations" %} For more information read the Washington Administrative Codes (WACs) related to these topics: * WAC 110-149-0130: Meeting the safety and well-being needs of children and youth * WAC 110-149-0140: Additional requirements for Indian children * WAC 110-149-0170: Medical consent {% endCallout %}
