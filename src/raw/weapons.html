--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: Weapons templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- Keep weapons in locked storage, and make sure the child or youth in your care cannot access them. If you need help buying items to secure weapons, contact your child or youth’s caseworker. Examples of weapons include:  * Guns * Ammunition * Other items designed to cause bodily harm or physical damage.
