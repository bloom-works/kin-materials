--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: Payments and reimbursements templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- If you’re a licensed kinship caregiver, you’ll get monthly foster care maintenance payments while caring for the child or youth. This support includes but is not limited to: * Money to cover gas and transportation costs. * Money to cover any property damage costs. * Vouchers to buy clothes for the child or youth (if you cannot get what the child or youth needs with the monthly foster care maintenance payment or through local community resources).
