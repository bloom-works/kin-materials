--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: License denials and suspensions templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- We’ll work with you before we deny, suspend, or cancel your kinship license.  We may deny, suspend, or cancel a kinship license in the following situations:  * You do not meet the licensing requirements.  * People in your home or on your property have not passed a background check.  * You did, allowed, or assisted in something illegal on the property of a home or facility that provides care to children or youth.   * You give us false information on purpose, and it affects the safety and well-being of the kinship child or youth. * You cannot support the safety, health, and well-being of the kinship child or youth.   * You cannot or will not support a child's or youth's cultural needs, including their needs  based on their race, ethnicity, religion, or sexual orientation and gender identity, and expression (SOGIE).   * We find that you abused, neglected, or abandoned a child or youth.   If we decide to deny, suspend, or cancel your license, we’ll send you a certified letter to let you know. The letter will say what you can do if you disagree with our decision.  DCYF makes decisions about:  * Applicants for licenses * Licenses we issue * Licensed kinship caregivers  {% Callout "related", "Related content" %} * License requirements * Background checks * Licensing special circumstances * Health and medicine * Food and clothing * Home conditions * Weapons * Discipline and restraint * Drugs and alcohol * Supervision and monitoring * Transportation * LGBTQ+ {% endCallout %} {% Callout "note", "Read the regulations" %} For more information read the Washington Administrative Codes (WACs) related to these topics: * WAC 110-149-0130: Meeting the safety and well-being needs of children and youth * WAC 110-149-0410: Denying, suspending, or revoking a license * WAC 110-149-0330: Kinship licensing process * WAC 110-149-0340: Background check requirements {% endCallout %}
