--- layout: layouts/raw.njk title: License denials, suspensions, and revocations permalink: /raw/license-denials-suspensions-revocations.html templateEngineOverride: md,njk parent_page: Kinship caregiver rules parent_page_link: rules.html --- We’ll work with you before we deny, suspend, or revoke your kinship license.  We may deny, suspend, or revoke a kinship license in the following situations:  * You do not meet the [licensing requirements](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/license-requirements/).  * People in your home or on your property have not passed a [background check](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/background-checks).  * You did, allowed, or assisted in something illegal on the property of a home or facility that provides care to children or youth.   * You give us false information on purpose, and it affects the safety and well-being of the kinship child or youth. * You cannot support the safety, health, and well-being of the kinship child or youth.   * You cannot or will not support a child's or youth's cultural needs, including their needs based on their [race, ethnicity, religion](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/race-ethnicity-religion-and-culture/), or [sexual orientation and gender identity, and expression](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/lgbtq/). * We find that you abused, neglected, or abandoned a child or youth.   If we decide to deny, suspend, or revoke your license, we’ll send you a certified letter to let you know. If you disagree with our decision, you can follow the instructions in the letter to [appeal it](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/hearings-and-appeals). If you apply for a license, and we find that you cannot provide for the safety, health, and well-being of the kinship child or youth, we have the right to:  * Deny your application * Revoke your license if you have one * Take other actions against you for not complying with rules for kinship caregivers {% Callout "related", "Related content" %} * [License requirements](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/license-requirements/) * [Background checks](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/background-checks) * [Licensing special circumstances](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/licensing-special-circumstances/) * [Health and medicine](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/healthcare-meds) * [Food and clothing](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/food-and-clothing) * [Home conditions](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/home-conditions/) * [Weapons](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/weapons/) * [Discipline and restraint](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/discipline-and-restraint) * [Drugs and alcohol](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/drugs-and-alcohol/) * [Supervision and monitoring](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/supervision-and-monitoring) * [Transportation](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/transportation) * [LGBTQ+](https://dcyf-kin-materials.netlify.app/raw/lgbtq/) {% endCallout %} {% Callout "note", "Read the regulations" %} For more information read the Washington Administrative Codes (WACs) related to these topics: * WAC 110-149-0130: Meeting the safety and well-being needs of children and youth * WAC 110-149-0410: Denying, suspending, or revoking a license * WAC 110-149-0330: Kinship licensing process * WAC 110-149-0340: Background check requirements {% endCallout %}
